The PSAP shall receive and process emergency calls in an NG911/i3 environment.
NG911 call data shall hand off into CAD for dispatch.
The CAD system shall create and update incidents in Computer-Aided Dispatch.
Dispatchers shall track unit status and assignments in CAD.
Field users shall access a mobile CAD client for field workflow.
The field app shall capture photos from the field device into the evidence workflow.
Body-worn camera evidence shall support chain of custody.
RMS shall provide records management for incident reports and evidence metadata.
EOC situational awareness tools shall support command coordination.
The system shall provide MCPTT group voice for mission-critical push-to-talk.
An LMR–MCX interworking function shall bridge radio and MCX domains.
A common operational map shall be shared across CAD and EOC.
IPAWS alerting shall originate or relay public warning messages.
Wireless Emergency Alerts (WEA) touchpoints shall be supported.
The LMR system shall support P25 Phase 1 trunked voice.
