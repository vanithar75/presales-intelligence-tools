The system shall provide MCPTT group voice for mission-critical push-to-talk.
MCVideo mission-critical video shall be supported for authorized users.
MCData shall provide mission-critical data services.
MCPTT emergency calling with priority preemption shall be supported.
An LMR–MCX interworking function shall bridge radio and MCX domains.
MCX affiliation shall manage membership in MCX groups.
MCPTT private calls shall be supported between authorized users.
Floor control / talker arbitration shall apply to MCPTT group calls.
MCX location reporting shall be available to authorized consumers.
MCData short data service (SDS) messages shall be supported.
MCData file distribution shall deliver files to groups or individuals.
MCX priority and preemption policies shall apply to critical traffic.
