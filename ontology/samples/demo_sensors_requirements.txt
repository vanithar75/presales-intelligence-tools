The agency shall deploy fixed CCTV cameras contributing video observations.
Live video shall be shared into CAD and EOC workflows.
Body-worn cameras shall support capture and evidence workflow.
A video management system (VMS) shall manage agency video streams and recordings.
Operators shall search and retrieve recorded video by time, camera, and incident.
Video retention, hold, and purge policies shall be enforced.
PTZ camera control shall be available from ops workstations.
Ops shall display multi-camera layouts on a video wall.
Body-worn evidence shall support upload, classification, and chain of custody.
Live video shall stream into the PSAP/CAD for situational awareness.
ALPR shall provide license plate reads linked to incidents.
ALPR hotlist matching shall alert ops in near real time.
Gunshot detection shall feed acoustic alerts into operations.
Gunshot alerts shall create or enrich CAD incidents.
Sensor alerts shall route to the correct ops queue by policy.
Traffic and roadway sensors shall enrich incident awareness.
Multi-sensor fusion shall correlate video, ALPR, and gunshot events.
Dispatchable UAS/drones shall operate as aerial sensors/units.
Drone video downlink shall display in ops/CAD workflows.
UAS geo-fence and airspace constraints shall be enforced.
