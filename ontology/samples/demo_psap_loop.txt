The PSAP shall receive and process emergency calls in an NG911/i3 environment.
Caller location shall be acquired via ALI/AML civic and geodetic methods.
Abandoned and silent 911 calls shall be detected and processed.
Location discrepancies between reported and verified locations shall be resolved.
NG911 call data shall hand off into CAD for dispatch.
The CAD system shall create and update incidents in Computer-Aided Dispatch.
Dispatchers shall track unit status and assignments in CAD.
The system shall recommend units based on location, type, and priority.
CAD shall display unit AVL on the dispatch map.
Secure messaging shall be available between dispatch and field units.
