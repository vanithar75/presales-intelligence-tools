The CAD system shall create and update incidents in Computer-Aided Dispatch.
Field users shall access a mobile CAD client for field workflow.
The field app shall capture and update incident details from a field mobile application.
Field narrative and electronic forms shall be linked to incidents.
Field apps shall operate offline and synchronize when connectivity returns.
Photos and video shall be captured from the field device into the evidence workflow.
Body-worn camera evidence shall support chain of custody upload and classify.
Body-worn camera media upload shall be confirmed from the field workflow.
RMS shall provide records management for incident reports and evidence metadata.
The RMS shall package case and incident records for prosecution and sharing.
Evidence and property-room custody records shall link to RMS cases.
EOC situational awareness / sitrep tools shall support command coordination.
A multi-source common operating picture shall be presented for EOC / C2 coordination.
Command and control (C2) supervisory views shall show active incidents and unit workload.
